About Sourabh

Sourabh Bhatia began his writing journey with novellas centred on resilience, healing, and introspection. A passionate reader with a deep interest in Indian history and mythology, he gradually felt inspired to write from his own perspective. His latest project, ‘Shri Ram Gaatha’, is a novel-style retelling of Maharshi Valmiki’s Ramayan, written with close attention to detail and a focus on the principles of dharma and purpose.

He is based in India and sees mythology as a living dialogue between past and present, not merely a historical record.

When he is not writing, he spends his time reading mythology, reflecting on philosophy, listening to music, or developing new story ideas.

Shri Ram Gaatha: Book 1 of 4

The Dawn of Dharma

2025

From the divine birth of Ram to the fateful day of his departure from Ayodhya, this book traces the beginning of his journey. Witness his early years, the blessings of sages, the breaking of Lord Shiva’s bow, and the joys of his marriage with Sita, culminating in the moment of parting when dharma first meets its trial. The book also explores characters outside Ayodhya who will play significant roles in the journey.

Shri Ram Gaatha: Book 2 of 4

The Exile of Dharma

2026

As Ram enters the forest in exile, the narrative deepens into themes of sacrifice, loyalty, and endurance. Along with highlighting Bharat’s devotion, this book follows the trio of Ram, Sita, and Lakshman as they journey through the vast wilderness of the South. From the quiet ashrams to the dark forests of Dandak, it builds towards a god-stating turn: the abduction of Sita by Raavan.
